AI impact on energy and water usage

8 takeaways from recent research

Version 1.0 by Jon Ippolito

It's hard to keep up with studies of the energy impact of generative AI, so here are 8 takeaways from the sources I have personally found most illuminating.

I'm aiming for a "just the facts" approach that sidesteps the dueling interpretations of AI champions and critics. I'm also using one set of measures rather than comparing apples to oranges, including:
